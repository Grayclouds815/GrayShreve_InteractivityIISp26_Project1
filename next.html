My Journey Through Illness

I have learned to be more empathetic and understanding towards others who are going through difficult times.
I have learned to be more resilient and to not give up easily.
I have learned to find joyin the small things in life and to appreciate the beauty.
I have learned to be more creative and to find new ways to express myself.
I have learned that being disabled does not define me and that I am still capable of living a full and meaningful life.
